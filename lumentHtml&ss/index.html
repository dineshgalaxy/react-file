LENDING IS MORE THAN A LOAN

WE MEASURE OUR RESULTS BY YOUR SUCCESS

At Lument, our commitment to relationships drives us to understand our clients’ business – and that insight guides us as we structure our loans. Thanks to the broad product platform we’ve assembled by combining Hunt Real Estate Capital, Lancaster Pollard, and RED Capital Group, our expert originators can choose the precise financing option that best meets your needs. At Lument, we are redefining what you can expect from a financial partner.

Expertise and experience make a difference

Our seasoned professionals deliver on what they promise, understand the importance of responsiveness, and are always one step ahead, assuring speed and certainty of execution.

Products that match your needs

With Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac, and HUD/FHA financing, along with bridge and mezzanine solutions from our strong balance sheet, we craft customized solutions that meet your evolving financing needs.

We make new customers repeat customers

We view every transaction – whether it’s your first or fifteenth – as an opportunity to go above and beyond for our customers. That’s how we earn your loyalty and repeat business.
